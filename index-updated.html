Welcome to Our Ministry

Destined for Greatness Messianic Ministries is dedicated to spreading the Gospel of Yeshua (Jesus) within the Jewish community and beyond. We are committed to bridging the gap between Jewish heritage and faith in Messiah, serving our community through education, charity, and spiritual growth.

"For we are God's handiwork, created in Messiah Yeshua to do good works, which God prepared in advance for us to do." - Ephesians 2:10

Our Core Values

📖

Biblical Foundation

Rooted in Torah and the New Testament, teaching the complete Scripture with Jewish cultural context.

🤝

Community Service

Serving the Jewish community and beyond through practical charity, education, and support programs.

🌟

Spiritual Growth

Nurturing believers in their faith journey and helping others discover the Messiah through love and truth.

What We Do

Our ministry operates through three main pillars:

  • Educational Programs - Kaloko New Life School serving students from preschool through adult education
  • Charitable Work - Supporting families in need within our community
  • Spiritual Ministry - Worship, counseling, and faith development
