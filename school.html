About the School

Kaloko New Life School was established with a vision to provide quality education rooted in Messianic Jewish faith — nurturing the whole child intellectually, spiritually, and culturally.

  • Hebrew Language Integration — Students develop fluency through immersion in daily instruction
  • Jewish Culture & Traditions — Celebrating holidays, studying Torah, and understanding our heritage
  • Messianic Faith Foundation — Biblical teaching that connects Old and New Testaments
  • Character Development — Mentoring in values, leadership, and service to others
  • Small Class Sizes — Personalised attention and genuine community
  • Experienced Educators — Passionate about both academics and faith formation

Grade Levels & Programs

A full spectrum of faith-based education from early childhood through adult learning.

🌱

Early Learning — Ages 3–5

Foundation in Hebrew, Jewish culture, Bible stories, and faith basics in a warm and nurturing environment.

📖

Elementary — Grades 1–6

Core academics with Hebrew language, Torah study, Jewish history, and character development.

🎓

Secondary — Grades 7–12

Advanced biblical studies, Hebrew literature, Jewish philosophy, and college preparation with faith integration.

🕯️

Adult Education

Evening classes and seminars on Messianic faith, Torah commentary, Hebrew language, and Jewish history.
