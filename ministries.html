📚Kaloko New Life School

Excellence in Faith-Based Jewish Education

Kaloko New Life School provides comprehensive biblical education integrated with Jewish heritage, language, and culture. We believe that quality education rooted in faith in Messiah creates thoughtful, compassionate, and purposeful leaders who honor their Jewish identity while embracing their faith in Yeshua.

Academic Excellence + Spiritual Formation

Our curriculum combines rigorous academics with spiritual development, Hebrew language instruction, and deep engagement with Jewish traditions and Messianic faith. Students graduate with strong academic foundations, cultural pride, and a living faith.

Grade Levels & Programs:

Early Learning (Ages 3-5)

Foundation in Hebrew, Jewish culture, Bible stories, and faith basics in a nurturing environment

Elementary (Grades 1-6)

Core academics with Hebrew language, Torah study, Jewish history, and character development

Secondary (Grades 7-12)

Advanced biblical studies, Hebrew literature, Jewish philosophy, and college preparation with faith integration

Adult Education

Evening classes and seminars on Messianic faith, Torah commentary, Hebrew language, and Jewish history

What Makes Kaloko New Life School Special:

  • Hebrew Language Integration: Students develop fluency through immersion in daily instruction
  • Jewish Culture & Traditions: Celebrating holidays, studying Torah, and understanding our heritage
  • Messianic Faith Foundation: Biblical teaching that connects Old and New Testaments
  • Character Development: Mentoring in values, leadership, and service to others
  • Small Class Sizes: Personalized attention and genuine community among students and teachers
  • Experienced Educators: Dedicated teachers passionate about both academics and faith formation

Extracurricular Activities & Events:

Youth Groups & Clubs

Holiday Celebrations

Passover Seders, Sukkot festivals, Hanukkah celebrations with spiritual significance

Students engaged in charitable work and community outreach projects

Summer and seasonal programs for spiritual growth and bonding

🤝Community Charitable Services

Meeting Real Needs with Compassion

Following the command to love our neighbors, our charity ministry provides practical assistance to families in need within our community. We believe faith without works is dead, and we're committed to demonstrating Messiah's love through service.

Our Services Include:

Food Assistance

Regular food distributions and meal support for families facing food insecurity

Financial Support

Emergency assistance for rent, utilities, and medical expenses

Family Services

Counseling, job training, and mentorship programs for families

Elderly Care

Special support and community for seniors in our Jewish population
